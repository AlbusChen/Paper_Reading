APWA: A Distributed Architecture for Parallelizable Agentic Workflows ★ 高度相关
Evan Rose, Tushin Mallick, Matthew D. Laws, Cristina Nita-Rotaru et al. · 2026-05-14
cs.MAcs.AI
APWA addresses multi-agent scaling bottlenecks by decomposing agentic workflows into non-interfering subproblems that can be processed in parallel without cross-communication. Supports heterogeneous data and parallel processing patterns across diverse domains. Demonstrates dynamic decomposition of complex queries and scales on tasks where prior systems fail completely.
中文摘要
APWA通过将智能体工作流分解为无干扰子问题来解决多智能体系统的扩展瓶颈，各子任务可并行处理且无需跨智能体通信。支持异构数据和多种并行处理模式。实验表明可动态分解复杂查询，在现有系统完全失败的大规模任务上仍能有效扩展，为通信高效的并行多智能体架构提供了新思路。

LEMON: Learning Executable Multi-Agent Orchestration via Counterfactual Reinforcement Learning ★ 高度相关
Xudong Chen, Yixin Liu, Hua Wei, Kaize Ding · 2026-05-14
cs.AIcs.MA
LEMON learns executable multi-agent orchestration specifications—covering role design, capacity assignment, and dependency structure—via counterfactual RL that isolates credit assignment for local decisions. Role, capacity, and dependency jointly determine both solution quality and execution efficiency. Achieves state-of-the-art on six reasoning and coding benchmarks.
中文摘要
LEMON通过反事实强化学习学习可执行的多智能体编排规范，联合优化角色设计、能力分配和依赖结构，并用反事实信号隔离局部决策的信用归因。研究揭示三者共同影响任务质量和执行效率。在六个推理和编码基准上达到最优性能，为自动化多智能体系统设计提供了学习方法。

Temporal Fair Division in Multi-Agent Systems: From Precise Alternation Metrics to Scalable Coordination Proxies ◆ 相关
Nikolaos Al. Papadopoulos · 2026-05-14
cs.GTcs.MA
Introduces formal metrics for temporal fairness in repeated multi-agent resource competition. Rotational Periodicity (RP) decomposes fairness into Rotational Score and Waiting Periods components, achieving 12-25x computational speedup over sliding-window ALT metrics. Key finding: Q-learning agents dramatically underperform random policies on fairness metrics despite high reward fairness.
中文摘要
为多智能体重复资源竞争引入时间公平性形式化度量。轮转周期性（RP）将公平性分解为轮转评分和等待期评估两个分量，相比滑动窗口ALT度量实现12-25倍计算加速。关键发现：Q学习智能体在奖励公平性高的情况下，时间公平性指标却大幅不如随机策略，揭示了现有MARL公平性的盲区。
