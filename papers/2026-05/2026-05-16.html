Beyond Individual Intelligence: Surveying Collaboration, Failure Attribution, and Self-Evolution in LLM-based Multi-Agent Systems ◆ 相关
Shihao Qi, Jie Ma, Rui Xing, Wei Guo et al. · 2026-05-16
multi-agent
A survey of LLM-based multi-agent systems arguing that individual-agent, collaboration, and self-evolution research have been studied in isolation, leaving their dependencies unexamined. It organizes the field around a causally linked 'LIFE progression' — Lay the capability foundation, Integrate agents through collaboration, Find faults through attribution, and Evolve through self-improvement — highlighting how tighter coordination amplifies hard-to-diagnose error propagation across agents and interaction rounds. It outlines open challenges and a research agenda for closed-loop systems capable of continuous diagnosis, structural reorganization, and behavior refinement.
中文摘要
本文系统综述了基于大语言模型的多智能体系统，指出现有研究将单体能力、协作机制与自我进化割裂看待，忽视了三者间的依赖关系。作者提出 'LIFE' 递进框架——奠定能力基础（Lay）、通过协作整合智能体（Integrate）、借助归因定位故障（Find）、经由自主改进实现进化（Evolve），并强调更紧密的协调会放大跨智能体、跨交互轮次且难以诊断的错误传播。文章梳理了开放挑战，并为支持持续诊断、结构重组与行为优化的闭环系统提出了研究议程。

Learning to Communicate Locally for Large-Scale Multi-Agent Pathfinding ◆ 相关
Valeriy Vyaltsev, Alsu Sagirova, Anton Andreychuk, Oleg Bulichev et al. · 2026-05-16
multi-agent
Decentralized learning-based multi-agent pathfinding (MAPF) solvers frame the problem as a Dec-POMDP but rarely add communication without sacrificing scalability. This paper introduces LC-MAPF, a generalizable pre-trained model with a learnable local communication module that performs multi-round message exchange between neighboring agents to share features and improve coordination. Across diverse unseen test scenarios it outperforms existing imitation- and reinforcement-learning MAPF solvers while preserving scalability, addressing a common bottleneck of communication-based methods.
中文摘要
去中心化的学习型多智能体路径规划（MAPF）通常将问题建模为 Dec-POMDP，但引入通信往往会牺牲可扩展性。本文提出 LC-MAPF，一种可泛化的预训练模型，配备可学习的局部通信模块，在相邻智能体间进行多轮信息交换以共享特征、提升协调能力。在多种未见测试场景下，该方法在各项指标上优于现有的模仿学习与强化学习MAPF 求解器，且未损害可扩展性，缓解了通信式方法的常见瓶颈。

STALE: Can LLM Agents Know When Their Memories Are No Longer Valid? · 低相关
Hanxiang Chao, Yihan Bai, Rui Sheng, Tianle Li et al. · 2026-05-16
Current LLM-agent memory benchmarks test static fact retrieval and overlook whether agents can revise stored beliefs when later evidence implicitly invalidates them — a failure mode the authors call 'Implicit Conflict.' They introduce STALE, a benchmark of 400 expert-validated conflict scenarios (1,200 queries, contexts up to 150K tokens) with a three-dimensional probing framework (State Resolution, Premise Resistance, Implicit Policy Adaptation), plus a CUPMem prototype using structured state consolidation. Even the best frontier model reaches only 55.2% accuracy, revealing a pervasive gap between retrieving updated evidence and acting on it.
中文摘要
现有大模型智能体记忆评测主要考察静态事实检索，忽视了当后续证据隐式推翻旧记忆时智能体能否更新已存信念——作者将此失效模式称为'隐式冲突'。本文提出 STALE 基准，包含 400 个经专家校验的冲突场景（1200 个查询，上下文长达 15 万 token），并设计三维探测框架（状态消解、前提抵抗、隐式策略适配），同时给出基于结构化状态整合的 CUPMem 原型。即便最强前沿模型整体准确率也仅为 55.2%，揭示了'检索到更新证据'与'据此采取行动'之间的普遍鸿沟。

EvolveMem:Self-Evolving Memory Architecture via AutoResearch for LLM Agents · 低相关
Jiaqi Liu, Xinyu Ye, Peng Xia, Zeyu Zheng et al. · 2026-05-16
Existing LLM-agent memory systems let stored content evolve but freeze the retrieval mechanism (scoring, fusion, answer policies) at deployment. EvolveMem exposes the full retrieval configuration as a structured action space optimized by an LLM-powered diagnosis module that reads per-question failure logs, identifies root causes, and proposes guarded configuration adjustments with automatic revert-on-regression and explore-on-stagnation safeguards — an autonomous 'AutoResearch' loop. It improves over the strongest baseline by 25.7% relative on LoCoMo and 18.9% on MemBench, with evolved configurations transferring positively across benchmarks.
中文摘要
现有大模型智能体记忆系统允许存储内容演化，却把检索机制（打分、融合、答案生成策略）在部署时冻结。EvolveMem 将完整的检索配置暴露为结构化动作空间，由 LLM 驱动的诊断模块读取逐题失败日志、定位根因并提出受控的配置调整，配合自动回退（性能回退时）与探索（停滞时）保护机制，形成自主的 'AutoResearch' 闭环。该方法在 LoCoMo 上相对最强基线提升 25.7%、在 MemBench 上提升 18.9%，且演化得到的配置能在不同基准间正向迁移。
