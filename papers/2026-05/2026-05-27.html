Beyond Final Answers: Auditing Trajectory-Level Hallucinations in Multi-Agent Industrial Workflows ◆ 相关
· 2026-05-27
multi-agent
Presents Trajel, a framework that audits multi-agent industrial workflows by detecting hallucinations within intermediate Thought-Action-Observation steps across five categories (factual, referential, logical, procedural, scope). Finds that nearly half of problematic trajectories contain multiple hallucination types and trajectory-level inspection substantially outperforms final-answer-only verification.
中文摘要
本文提出 Trajel 框架，针对多智能体工业工作流中只看最终输出的传统幻觉评测短板，对 Thought-Action-Observation 链路上的事实/指代/逻辑/流程/范围五类幻觉进行细粒度审计。研究发现近半问题轨迹同时包含多种幻觉类型，且基于完整轨迹的检测显著优于仅做事后答案验证，为工业级 Agent 可靠性评估提供了新基线。

DarkForest: Less Talk, Higher Accuracy for Multi-Agent LLMs ◆ 相关
· 2026-05-27
multi-agent
Proposes DarkForest, a multi-agent LLM coordination framework that keeps agents independent (no inter-agent chatter) and uses a coordinator with calibrated belief estimation over clustered candidate answers. Across six reasoning benchmarks, it improves the strongest baseline by up to 30.7% while cutting token consumption by up to 6.5x versus communication-heavy baselines.
中文摘要
本文提出 DarkForest，一种受控通信的多智能体协作框架：智能体先独立作答，再由协调器基于结构化候选聚类和可靠性加权的置信分布做最终判断，从根本上避免错误推理在通信中传播放大。在六个推理基准上，DarkForest 相对最强基线提升至多 30.7%，同时通信 token 消耗最多降低 6.5 倍，是多智能体效率方向的重要工作。
