π-Bench: Evaluating Proactive Personal Assistant Agents in Long-Horizon Workflows ◆ 相关
· 2026-05-24
curated
Introduces pi-Bench, a benchmark of 100 multi-turn tasks across 5 personas that explicitly tests whether personal-assistant agents can surface hidden user intents, carry context across sessions, and act on unstated constraints. Results show proactivity is largely orthogonal to raw task-completion ability and that prior interaction history meaningfully helps later proactive intent resolution.
中文摘要
面向个人助理智能体的“主动协助”能力盲区，提出 pi-Bench：包含 5 类人物画像、100 个长对话多轮任务，显式注入隐藏意图、跨任务依赖与跨会话延续，以同时衡量任务完成度与主动性。实验表明：主动协助仍是难题，主动性与任务完成度并不等价，且历史交互信息对后续主动意图推断有显著价值。

ACC: Compiling Agent Trajectories for Long-Context Training ◆ 相关
· 2026-05-24
curated
Observes that standard agent SFT masks tool responses, wasting the long-context reasoning signal scattered across multi-turn trajectories. ACC compiles search/SWE/DB agent trajectories into long-context QA pairs that combine the original question with all gathered observations, providing direct supervision for cross-turn reasoning; Qwen3-30B-A3B trained with ACC reaches 68.3 on MRCR (+18.1) and 77.5 on GraphWalks (+7.6), matching a 235B-A22B model while preserving general capability.
中文摘要
指出标准智能体 SFT 屏蔽了工具返回，导致多轮轨迹中分散的长上下文证据信号被浪费。ACC 将搜索、软件工程、数据库等智能体轨迹编译为长上下文 QA 对——把原始问题与跨轮工具响应/观察拼合，让模型在不调用工具的情况下直接作答，从而显式监督长程跨段推理。Qwen3-30B-A3B 用 ACC 训练后在 MRCR 达 68.3 (+18.1)、GraphWalks 达 77.5 (+7.6)，效果对齐 235B-A22B，同时保留 GPQA/MMLU-Pro/AIME/IFEval 等通用能力。

Forecasting Scientific Progress with Artificial Intelligence ◆ 相关
· 2026-05-24
curated
Introduces CUSP, a cutoff-controlled benchmark of 4,760 scientific events that tests frontier models on feasibility, mechanism, generative solution design, and timing of future scientific advances. Models can spot plausible directions but unreliably predict realization and badly misestimate timing; performance is largely insensitive to training cutoff and shows overconfidence and strong response biases - suggesting today's AI is not yet a dependable forecasting tool for science.
中文摘要
构建 CUSP：覆盖 4,760 个科学事件、按训练截止时间严格分桶的多学科基准，从可行性判断、机制推理、生成式方案设计与时间预测四个维度评估前沿模型的科学预测能力。结果显示模型能识别合理方向，却难以可靠预测成果是否会落地、并系统性误估时间；性能对事件是否在训练截止之后不敏感，且存在过度自信与显著响应偏差，表明当前 AI 还远未成为可靠的科学进展预测工具。

Maestro: Reinforcement Learning to Orchestrate Hierarchical Model-Skill Ensembles ◆ 相关
· 2026-05-24
curated
Frames multimodal task solving as RL orchestration over a hierarchical model-skill registry: a lightweight 4B policy decides at each step whether to call an external expert and which model-skill pair to invoke, trained only with outcome rewards. Maestro reaches 70.1% average across ten benchmarks - beating GPT-5 and Gemini-2.5-Pro - and the learned orchestration zero-shot generalizes to unseen experts plugged into the registry.
中文摘要
将异构多模态任务建模为对“模型-技能”分层注册表的序贯决策：Maestro 用一个 4B 轻量策略动态组合冻结的专家模型与两层技能库，仅以结果奖励进行 RL 训练，决定何时调用外部专家、调用哪个模型-技能对以及何时终止。在十个多模态基准上平均 70.1%，超过 GPT-5 (69.3%) 与 Gemini-2.5-Pro (68.7%)；新增训练外的专家也能零样本组合使用，仍胜过闭源基线。
