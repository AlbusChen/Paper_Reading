Qwen-VLA: Unifying Vision-Language-Action Modeling across Tasks, Environments, and Robot Embodiments · 低相关
Qiuyue Wang, Mingsheng Li, Jian Guan, Jinhui Ye et al. · 2026-05-28
cs.ROcs.AIcs.CLTech Report
Qwen-VLA, from the Qwen team, is a unified vision-language-action framework that scales across diverse tasks, environments, and robot embodiments. Built on the Qwen vision-language backbone with a flow-matching action expert that maps multimodal observations and natural-language instructions to continuous control, it is trained via a three-stage curriculum (large-scale cross-embodiment pretraining, domain alignment, and task-specific adaptation) for strong, data-efficient generalization. On simulated and real-robot manipulation and navigation benchmarks it outperforms strong baselines on both in-distribution and out-of-distribution tasks, and the authors release model weights, training code, and evaluation protocols.
中文摘要
Qwen-VLA 是 Qwen 团队提出的统一视觉-语言-动作（VLA）框架，可在多样的任务、环境与机器人本体之间扩展。它基于 Qwen 视觉-语言主干，并加入通过流匹配训练的动作专家，将多模态观测与自然语言指令映射为连续控制信号；通过三阶段训练课程（大规模跨本体预训练、领域对齐、任务特定适配）实现强且数据高效的泛化。在仿真与真实机器人的操作和导航基准上，它在分布内与分布外任务上均超越强基线，作者并开源了模型权重、训练代码与评测协议。

When Cloud Agents Meet Device Agents: Lessons from Hybrid Multi-Agent Systems ◆ 相关
Corrado Rainone, Davide Belli, Bence Major, Arash Behboodi · 2026-05-28
cs.MAcs.AImulti-agent
This paper systematically studies the design space of hybrid multi-agent systems that combine cost-efficient on-device small language models with cloud-hosted frontier LLMs, where task accuracy, monetary cost, and edge energy are tightly coupled. The authors adapt two representative multi-agent architectures to support hybrid inference and analyze how individual design choices move the operating point along the power-cost-performance Pareto frontier. They find that small models can benefit substantially from LLM assistance, but the optimal architecture is highly task-dependent and more frontier-level compute does not consistently improve performance.
中文摘要
本文系统性地研究了混合多智能体系统的设计空间——将低成本的端侧小语言模型与云端前沿大模型相结合，其中任务准确率、金钱成本与边缘能耗紧密耦合。作者将两种代表性的多智能体架构改造为支持混合推理，并分析各项设计选择如何在“功耗-成本-性能”的帕累托前沿上移动工作点。研究发现：小模型能从大模型的协助中显著获益，但最优架构高度依赖于具体任务，而且更多的前沿算力并不总能带来更好的性能。

Chenghao Zhang, Guanting Dong, Yufan Liu, Tong Zhao et al. · 2026-05-28
cs.CLcs.AImulti-agent
This work introduces Ptah, a multi-agent harness for verifiable multimodal deep research that produces interleaved text-and-image reports. Specialized agents handle planning, research, and writing—constructing visual-aware plans, collecting claim-grounded evidence, maintaining a "Visual Working Memory" of source-aligned images, and composing reports via declarative multimodal tool use—while a verifier agent acts as an acceptance function enforcing factual grounding, citation fidelity, and cross-modal consistency. With a companion evaluation protocol (PtahEval), Ptah yields more reliable and visually informative reports than strong baselines.

Discovering Cooperative Pipelines: Autoresearch for Sequential Social Dilemmas ◆ 相关
Víctor Gallego · 2026-05-28
cs.MAcs.AIcs.LGmulti-agent
This paper proposes a two-level "autoresearch" approach to cooperation in multi-agent Sequential Social Dilemmas: an outer-loop researcher agent autonomously rewrites the inner-loop pipeline (system prompts, feedback functions, helper code, and iteration logic) of an LLM policy-synthesis system, runs evaluations, and keeps what works. Across two social-dilemma games, two synthesizer LLMs, and two welfare objectives (utilitarian efficiency and Rawlsian maximin), the researcher agent reliably beats hand-designed and prompt-only baselines and sharply reduces run-to-run variance. Notably, the discovered pipelines are objective-dependent—an explicit fairness mechanism emerges only under the maximin objective—supporting an information-design reading of what the researcher reveals to the synthesizer.
中文摘要
本文提出一种用于多智能体“序贯社会困境”（SSD）中促进合作的两层“自动研究”方法：外层的研究者智能体自主改写内层 LLM 策略合成系统的流水线（系统提示、反馈函数、辅助代码与迭代逻辑），运行评测并保留有效改动。在两个社会困境游戏、两个合成器大模型以及两种福利目标（功利主义效率与罗尔斯最大化最小值）下，该研究者智能体稳定地超越了手工设计与仅依赖提示的基线，并大幅降低了运行间的方差。值得注意的是，所发现的流水线依赖于目标——只有在 maximin 目标下才会自发引入显式的公平机制——这支持了一种关于“研究者向合成器揭示什么信息”的信息设计解读。

AsyncTool: Evaluating the Asynchronous Function Calling Capability under Multi-Task Scenarios ◆ 相关
Kou Shi, Ziao Zhang, Shiting Huang, Avery Nie et al. · 2026-05-27
cs.AI
AsyncTool is a benchmark and evaluation protocol for the asynchronous function-calling ability of LLM agents, motivated by the fact that most agents invoke tools sequentially even when calls could run concurrently. It measures whether models can identify independent tool calls that may be dispatched in parallel, correctly manage dependencies between calls, and aggregate asynchronous results. Across proprietary and open models, the authors find that while strong models can plan parallel calls, they frequently mishandle inter-call dependencies, revealing a gap between sequential and truly asynchronous tool use.
中文摘要
AsyncTool 是一个评测 LLM 智能体异步函数调用能力的基准与协议，其动机在于：即便多个工具调用本可并发执行，现有智能体大多仍按顺序调用，白白浪费了延迟。它衡量模型能否识别可并行分派的独立调用、正确管理调用之间的依赖关系，并聚合异步返回的结果。在多个闭源与开源模型上，作者发现：强模型虽能规划并行调用，却常常处理不好调用间的依赖，揭示了“顺序”与“真正异步”工具调用之间的差距。

CoHyDE: Iterative Co-Training of LLM Rewriter & Dense Encoder for Tool Retrieval ◆ 相关
Vaishali Senthil, Ashutosh Hathidara, Sebastian Schreiber · 2026-05-28
cs.AIcs.IRcs.LG
CoHyDE addresses tool retrieval for agents operating over large tool libraries by iteratively co-training an LLM query rewriter and a dense encoder. The rewriter expands a user query into hypothetical tool-use descriptions while the dense encoder matches them against tool documentation, and the two are trained in alternating rounds so each improves the other. On large-scale tool-retrieval benchmarks, CoHyDE improves both retrieval accuracy and downstream task success over strong retrieval baselines.
中文摘要
CoHyDE 针对智能体在大规模工具库上的“工具检索”问题，提出迭代式地协同训练一个 LLM 查询改写器与一个稠密编码器。改写器将用户查询扩展为假设性的工具使用描述，稠密编码器则将其与工具文档进行匹配；两者交替训练、互相促进。在大规模工具检索基准上，CoHyDE 在检索准确率与下游任务成功率上均优于强检索基线。

PANDO: Efficient Multimodal AI Agents via Online Skill Distillation ◆ 相关
Yubo Li, Yidi Miao, Yuntian Shen, Yuxin Liu · 2026-05-24
cs.AI
PANDO builds efficient multimodal AI agents through online skill distillation: instead of querying a large multimodal model for every decision, it maintains a lightweight student agent that learns reusable skills on-the-fly from a stronger teacher, escalating to the teacher only on novel or low-confidence situations via a confidence-gated routing mechanism, and caching distilled skills for reuse. Across web-navigation, GUI-control, and embodied-manipulation benchmarks, PANDO substantially reduces expensive teacher calls while retaining most of the teacher's task success.
中文摘要
PANDO 通过“在线技能蒸馏”构建高效的多模态 AI 智能体：它不再对每个决策都调用大型多模态模型，而是维护一个轻量级学生智能体，在线地从更强的教师智能体学习可复用技能，仅在遇到新颖或低置信度情形时才通过“置信度门控路由”升级求助教师，并将蒸馏得到的技能缓存以备复用。在网页导航、GUI 操作与具身操作等基准上，PANDO 大幅减少了昂贵的教师调用，同时保留了教师的大部分任务成功率。
