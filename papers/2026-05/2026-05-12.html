Predictive Maps of Multi-Agent Reasoning: A Successor-Representation Spectrum for LLM Communication Topologies ★ 高度相关
Ethan David James Park, Dalal Alharthi · 2026-05-12
cs.MAcs.AIcs.LGmulti-agent
Introduces a spectral diagnostic for multi-agent LLM communication topologies using the successor representation. Analyzes chain, star, and mesh graphs and finds that the condition number perfectly predicts perturbation robustness (ρ_s=1.0) and spectral radius perfectly predicts error accumulation (ρ_s=−1.0). Proposes an affine-noise extension to handle non-contracting bias drift.
中文摘要
提出基于后继表示（Successor Representation）的多智能体LLM通信拓扑结构诊断方法。对链式、星型、网状三种拓扑进行谱分析，发现条件数（κ(M)）能完美预测系统对扰动的鲁棒性（r_s=1.0），谱半径能完美预测累积错误（r_s=−1.0）。本文是首个面向多智能体LLM系统的结构性漂移感知诊断工具，对通信拓扑设计有重要指导意义。

Takahiro Suzuki, Yuma Tamura, Keisuke Okumura · 2026-05-12

Studies Distance-r Independent Unlabeled MAPF, where agents must stay at pairwise distance ≥ r+1 at all times—a harder coordination constraint than standard collision avoidance. Proves feasibility is PSPACE-complete, then offers two algorithms (reduction-based optimal and configuration generator search) that handle hundreds of agents within practical runtimes.
中文摘要

Yue Deng, Zirui Wang, Yin Zhang · 2026-05-12
cs.LGcs.MAmulti-agent
Addresses the bias-variance tradeoff in value estimation for cooperative MARL, where large joint action spaces make policy distribution computation infeasible. Proposes Adaptive TD(λ) using a parametric likelihood-free density ratio estimator with dual replay buffers to weight state-action pairs by their likelihood under the current policy. Tested on QMIX and MAPPO in SMAC and Google Football.
中文摘要
