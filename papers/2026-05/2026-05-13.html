When Does Hierarchy Help? Benchmarking Agent Coordination in Event-Driven Industrial Scheduling ★ 高度相关
Ziqi Wang, Yuhao Yang, Zhiwei Ling, Wenzhuo Qian et al. · 2026-05-13
cs.MAcs.AImulti-agentagent coordination
DESBench benchmarks four agent coordination paradigms—centralized, hierarchical, heterarchical, and holonic—on discrete-event industrial scheduling with shared, dynamically evolving constraints. Key finding: centralized is robust but unscalable; hierarchical decomposes efficiently but suffers cross-level misalignment; heterarchical is flexible at high communication cost; holonic satisfies constraints at the cost of global robustness. Coordination architecture fundamentally determines system behavior.
中文摘要
提出DESBench基准，在离散事件工业调度场景中系统评测四种多智能体协调范式——集中式、层次式、异质式和全息式。核心发现：集中式鲁棒但不可扩展；层次式通过分解提升效率但存在跨层错位；异质式灵活但通信成本高；全息式满足约束但牺牲全局鲁棒性。协调架构的选择从根本上决定了系统行为，为多智能体系统设计提供了重要参考。

Finding the Weakest Link: Adversarial Attack against Multi-Agent Communications ★ 高度相关
Maxwell Standen, Junae Kim, Claudia Szabo · 2026-05-13
cs.LGcs.MAmulti-agentagent communication
Studies adversarial attacks targeting multi-agent communication in MARL systems. Uses Jacobian-based gradient information to identify which messages, agents, and timesteps are most vulnerable. Proposes two adversarial loss functions trading off attack success vs. impact. Demonstrates that targeted victim/message selection outperforms random selection in half of 30 tested scenarios, revealing critical communication security vulnerabilities in MAS.
中文摘要
针对多智能体强化学习（MARL）系统的通信通道研究对抗攻击。利用Jacobian梯度信息识别最易受攻击的消息、智能体和时间步，提出两种在攻击成功率与攻击影响之间权衡的对抗损失函数。在导航、捕食者-猎物和交通路口三个环境中验证，揭示了多智能体通信的关键安全漏洞，对通信协议设计有重要安全启示。

TacoMAS co-evolves both topology and capability in LLM-based MAS during inference, on different timescales: capabilities update fast (trajectory-level feedback) while topology evolves slowly (meta-LLM-driven agent birth/death operations).
