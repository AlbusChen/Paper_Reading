Streaming Communication in Multi-Agent Reasoning ★ 高度相关
Zhen Yang, Xiaogang Xu, Wen Wang, Cong Chen et al. · 2026-06-04
cs.CLcs.AIcs.MAmulti-agentcommunication efficiencymulti-agent efficiency
This paper targets the latency and error-propagation costs of multi-agent reasoning pipelines. It proposes StreamMA, where agents stream intermediate reasoning steps to downstream agents instead of waiting for complete chains, and reports better accuracy and lower latency across math, science, and code benchmarks on Chain, Tree, and Graph topologies.
中文摘要
本文针对多智能体推理中“生成完再传递”带来的线性延迟和错误累积问题，提出 StreamMA，将中间推理步骤实时流式传给下游智能体。实验覆盖数学、科学与代码任务以及链式、树状和图状拓扑，显示该方法同时提升准确率并降低端到端延迟。

Zhenting Qi, Huangyuan Su, Ao Qu, Chenyu Wang et al. · 2026-06-04

This work studies decentralized coordination for multi-agent intelligence through market-like mechanisms rather than hand-designed communication protocols. Agents bid for the right to act, exchange payments, and evolve through wealth-based selection; the resulting economy produces emergent multi-step strategies and outperforms monolithic baselines on five agentic tasks.

Token Budgets: An Empirical Catalog of 63 LLM-Agent Budget-Overrun Incidents, with an Affine-Typed Rust Mitigation as a Case Study ★ 高度相关
Sajjad Khan · 2026-06-04
cs.SEcs.MAcs.PLmulti-agent efficiency
This paper studies token and dollar budget overruns as a production failure mode for LLM agents, with a catalog of 63 incidents across 21 frameworks. It proposes an affine-typed Rust budget primitive that makes budget cloning, double spending, and use-after-delegation compile-time errors, with particular value for multi-agent delegation fanout races.
中文摘要
本文将 LLM 智能体的 token 和费用超支视为生产系统中的失效模式，整理了 21 个编排框架中的 63 起真实事件。作者提出基于仿射所有权的 Rust 预算原语，使预算复制、重复消费和委托后继续使用在编译期被拒绝，尤其适用于多智能体委托分叉导致的预算竞态。

Yingqi Zhang · 2026-06-04
cs.OScs.AIcs.CR
Agent libOS addresses operational control for long-running LLM agents rather than planner accuracy. It treats agents as process-like entities with lifecycle state, typed memory, checkpoints, explicit capabilities, human approval queues, and audit records, making runtime primitives the authority boundary for tool and side-effect access.
中文摘要
本文提出 Agent libOS，将长期运行的 LLM 智能体抽象为类似操作系统进程的执行主体，包含生命周期、对象记忆、检查点、能力权限和审计记录。其重点不是提升规划能力，而是通过运行时原语统一管控工具调用、外部副作用和人工授权，增强智能体系统的可恢复性与可审计性。
